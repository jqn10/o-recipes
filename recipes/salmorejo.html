What is Salmorejo

Salmorejo, sometimes known as ardoria or ardorío, is a

traditional soup originating from Andalusia, southern Spain,

made of tomato, bread, extra virgin olive oil and garlic.

Normally, the tomatoes are skinned and then puréed with the

other ingredients. The salmorejo is served cold and may be

garnished with diced Spanish serrano ham and boiled eggs.
