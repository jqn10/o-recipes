Homemade tortilla

This is our version of a tortilla, it is a homemade recipe that can be made

with meat or vegetarian. The whole process takes around 10-15 minutes.

It is served best with tortilla bread and with fresh orange juice as your drink.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Big Tomato
  • Half a Big Onion
  • Sausages (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika

Now that we have all of our ingredients, let's take a look at the procedure

Recipe

  1. Slice or dice the tomato and onion.
  2. Sautee them in the pan
